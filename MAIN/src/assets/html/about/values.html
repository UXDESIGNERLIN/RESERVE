MySpotBook offers booking systems for businesses who organize events, classes, activities, etc... Businesses can benefit from MySpotBook by merging widgets generated from MySpotBook admin into their official websites or share links on their social media. Customers of the businesses can reserve events within a short time without going through lots of signing up forms. Behind the scenes, businesses can analyze and track their events as well as contacting their customers within a few clicks without checking their emails one by one. Last but not least there are a bunch of other special features MySpotBook offers, like automatic confirmations, statistics, users tracking, and many more to come.

Time is the most precious thing we have as living beings. Therefore, we aim at offering services that automate most of the repeating must-do tasks. With our simple and efficient service, our users can increase their productivity.

As a start-up, we are more interested in long term cooperations rather than immediate benefits, we want to build a great community and grow together.
We love partnerships, and we believe the friends of our friends, are indeed our friends.

The team behind MySpotBook love their jobs, but they also love going out, joining events, having fun & starting new life adventures.
That's why MySpotBook puts a strong value in achieving success through balance. That makes for happier, more independent, more productive, more creative, and overall better individuals.
