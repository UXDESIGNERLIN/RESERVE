Our core values

This is what our company really care about <3

  • Simplicity
  • Community
  • Life & Work Balance

We want to help our costumers make their lives easier, that's why we love simplicity. A good tool solves something simple, is simple to understand and when tied together with a bunch of other simple tools (in a simple way) makes great products.
That's our philosophy.

We don't look for immediate benefits from our users, but for long term cooperations, building a great community and growing together with them.
We love partnerships, and we believe the friends of our friends, are indeed our friends.

We love our job, but we also love going out, joining activities, having fun & starting new life adventures.
That's why our company puts a strong value in achieving success throught balance.
That makes for happier. more independent, more productive, more creative, and overall better individuals.
